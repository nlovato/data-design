User Case:

Title: Adding merchandise to a wishlist

Description: Harley wants to add accesories & apparel to her wishlist.

Name of User & their role: Harley, madly in love with 'The Joker' & enjoys terrorizing Gotham City.

Usage Preconditions: Must have Amazon account & be logged in.

Usage Postconditions: Merchandise saved to wishlist & can be easily viewed.

Interaction Flow

Interaction Flow:

Interaction Flow:

  1. Harley enters www.amazon.com into her web browser.
  2. Server returns Amazon's main web page.
  3. Harley selects profile to access "Wishlist" & sees that her wishlist is empty.
    4. Harley adds an accessory along with a few apparel items to wishlist.
  4. Harley then sees her selected items in her wishlist.
  5. Server loads Harley's wishlist page on her Amazon account.
